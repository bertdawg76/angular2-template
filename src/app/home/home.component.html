The Pink Series was established for girl runners(the gentlemen can run with us but aren't eligable for awards). Our goal is to provide a women's retreat where women can run an awesome event and then party after! The Pink Series continues to grow each year and is Utah's largest womens only event, and race party.

SUPPORT THE CAUSE BY "LIFTING HEARTS"

With a common goal to be healthy survivors and to help other survivors heal, the group Lifting Hearts was organized to provide mentoring, monthly support meetings, breast cancer retreats, service opportunities, and family activities, all aimed at sharing support, wisdom, laughter and hope. Each woman who joins Lifting Hearts gains instant friendships with women who have walked in her path. She finds a place to celebrate today and figure out tomorrow, whatever comes. Become a part of the cause.
